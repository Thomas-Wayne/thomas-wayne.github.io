Upcoming Events

Cycle the Continental Divide

Join us for our annual Cycle the Continental Divide event, Monday May 21st - Saturday May 26th. The event is a rain or shine event so dress appropriately. There is a non-refundable registration fee of $200 for each participant and registration forms must be recieved on or before Saturday May 5th. This years route will have us exploring the 517.6 mile Idaho Hot Springs Mountain Bike Route. We will begin our journey in Idaho City, Idaho. The itinerary, maps of the trail, and a list of recommended items will be provided as part of a welcome package that will be shipped to you prior to the event.
